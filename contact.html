Types of Exercise

Aerobic (Cardio) Exercise

Aerobic exercises make your heart and lungs work harder to improve endurance and burn calories These exercises increase your breathing. Doing them regularly helps your body get stronger and more energetic. Example of Aerobic exercise is running, walking, swimming and cycling

Strength or Resistance Exercise

Strength exercises help build muscles and make your body stronger. These activities, like lifting weights or doing push-ups, make your muscles and bones stronger and improve your posture. Mostly doing this exercise is body builder

Flexibility Exercise

Flexibility exercises stretch your muscles and help your body move more easily. Activities like yoga or stretching improve the range of motion in your joints and reduce the chance of injury.

Balance Exercise

Balance and coordination exercises help your body stay stable and controlled during movement. They train your muscles and brain to work together so you can stand, walk, and move without easily losing balance.These exercises are important for preventing falls, improving posture, and helping you move safely in daily activities like walking, climbing stairs, or carrying objects.

Each type of exercise has a specific purpose some improve heart health, some build strength, some increase flexibility, and some improve balance.
